Welcome to learn-neurocompute's documentation!
==============================================
A collection of resources to support the learning of a variety of computing skills and computational concepts that are relevant to neuroimaging and neuroscience.

Initially developed to provide learning support to the course **Computing for Neuroimagers** (`[SLHS 497/597] <https://d2l.arizona.edu/d2l/home/924931/>`__ ) run at the University of Arizona in Fall 2020.
